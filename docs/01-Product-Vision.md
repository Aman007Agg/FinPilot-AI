# Product Vision

## Project Name

FinPilot AI

## Vision

To build a modern AI-powered Personal Finance Management platform that helps users track expenses, manage budgets, gain financial insights, and leverage AI to make smarter financial decisions.

## Objectives

- Expense Tracking
- Budget Management
- AI Assistant
- Financial Dashboard
- Receipt OCR
- Cloud Native Deployment

## Target Users

- Students
- Working Professionals
- Freelancers
- Small Business Owners
